Pin SizeUp to your toolbar

Pinning keeps SizeUp one click away — so you can add a family member's sizes and check fits without digging through menus.

SizeUp
Another extension
  1. Click the puzzle-piece (Extensions) icon at the top-right of Chrome.
  2. Find SizeUp in the list.
  3. Click the pin icon so SizeUp stays in your toolbar.
Then click the SizeUp icon any time to add a family member and start finding fits.
